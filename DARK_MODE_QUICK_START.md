### When to Switch

**Switch to Light Mode when:**
- Working during the day
- In a brightly lit room
- Sharing your screen in a meeting
- Printing or exporting screenshots

**Switch to Dark Mode when:**
- Working at night or early morning
- In a dim environment
- Experiencing eye strain
- Working for extended periods

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Theme Not Changing?

1. **Make sure you clicked the button** - Look for visual feedback in the status bar
2. **Restart the app** - In rare cases, a restart may be needed
3. **Check for updates** - Ensure you have the latest version

### Theme Resets to Light Mode?

- This is the default behavior on first launch
- After you toggle once, your preference will be saved
- Future launches will use your chosen theme

### Colors Look Wrong?

- Ensure you're using the latest version of the application
- Check your display settings (color calibration, night mode, etc.)
- Try switching themes back and forth
